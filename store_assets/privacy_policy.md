1. **Service Provision:** Matching customers with workers
2. **Identity Verification:** Ensuring safety and trust
3. **Payment Processing:** Handling transactions securely
4. **Communication:** Facilitating customer-worker contact
5. **Safety:** Emergency features and incident reporting

### Secondary Purposes:
- Improving our services
- Analytics and research
- Fraud prevention
- Legal compliance
- Marketing (with consent)

## Legal Basis for Processing

Under Sri Lankan Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and GDPR:

- **Contract Performance:** Processing necessary to provide services
- **Legal Obligation:** Compliance with laws and regulations
- **Legitimate Interests:** Safety, security, and service improvement
- **Consent:** Marketing communications and optional features

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption:** All data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
- **Regular Audits:** Security assessments and penetration testing
- **Employee Training:** Data protection awareness
- **Incident Response:** Breach notification procedures

## Data Retention

We retain your data for:

- **Active Accounts:** Duration of account activity
- **Inactive Accounts:** 2 years after last activity
- **Transaction Records:** 7 years (legal requirement)
- **Deleted Accounts:** 30 days (then permanently erased)

## Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

1. **Access:** Request copies of your personal data
2. **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate information
3. **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data
4. **Restriction:** Limit processing of your data
5. **Portability:** Receive data in machine-readable format
6. **Objection:** Object to certain processing activities
7. **Withdraw Consent:** Opt-out of optional processing

### To Exercise Your Rights:
